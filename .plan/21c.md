Flag back to #21a rather than special-casing — otherwise the "one path" invariant erodes.

Confirm a documented validation walkthrough is acceptable as the acceptance evidence.
- **History window vs lossless (shared with #21a).** Same concern as #21a: a reverse *migration* must pull all history (`"all"` semantics), not the default 90d window, or old Linear `Done` issues are silently dropped. Ensure the engine's lossless guarantee covers the `linear` case too.
